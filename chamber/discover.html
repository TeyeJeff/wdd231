Did You Know?

Accra is one of the fastest-growing cities in West Africa, attracting significant foreign investment in technology, finance, and logistics.

The city's economy is heavily influenced by the diverse commercial activity found in places like Makola Market and the modern commercial centers along Oxford Street.
